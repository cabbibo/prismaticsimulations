blarp



Summary

Blarp is a physical game based on touching and swinging. Guide your blarp around the kelp forest as you are chased by an ominous enemy. Collect targets to increase your score, orbs to grow your tail and colorful dots to change your whole reality!

The chase gets faster and faster, until you finally are tagged, and get to begin again.

Description

Tired of lo-poly graphics and pastel colors? try a casual game with visuals that are truly unique.

Born from the indie VR hit BLARP!, blarp takes the core mechanics of swinging, touching and flinging to mobile for a compelling and zen like experience. By using cutting edge graphics and the power of modern GPUs, blarp creates an mobile game unlike any other. If you enjoy Thumper, Rez, Flow, or any other game that cares more about being beautiful than being realistic, blarp just might be a game for you!

Description

Tired of the puppy eyes and cat ears, we set out to make a face filter app that is truly unique.


Face! Plant! uses cutting edge technology like Compute Shaders and Apple's True Depth camera to make an app that is playful,elegant and enthralling. Created as Prismatic Simulations flagship app, Face! Plant! takes an everyday occurance and makes it extroidinary. Use over 30 lenses that range from realistic to pyschedelic to be record videos and share anywhere you would your photos.


50% of proceeds will go directly to the Rainforest Action Network's Climate Action Fund. Plants are special and so are Faces. We want to support those who are fighting for both.
